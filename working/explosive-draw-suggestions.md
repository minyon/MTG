# explosive-draw — Card Suggestions

Commander: Niv-Mizzet, Visionary (UR)
Theme: Cast instants/sorceries, draw cards, make tokens and deal damage.

## Mana Rocks

- Commander's Sphere
- Izzet Locket
- Darksteel Ingot
- Worn Powerstone
- Hedron Archive

## Lands

- Command Tower

- Sulfur Falls

- Evolving Wilds
- 14x Island
- 8x Mountain

## Spell Payoff Creatures

- Guttersnipe — deals 2 damage whenever you cast an instant or sorcery
- Young Pyromancer — makes a 1/1 token whenever you cast an instant or sorcery
- Murmuring Mystic — makes a 1/1 flying Bird token for each instant/sorcery
- Crackling Drake — draws a card on entry, grows with instants/sorceries in graveyard
- Sprite Dragon — grows with each noncreature spell cast
- Melek, Izzet Paragon — copies the first instant/sorcery you cast from the top of your library
