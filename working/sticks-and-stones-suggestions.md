# sticks-and-stones — Card Suggestions

Commander: Old Stickfingers (BG)
Theme: Fill your graveyard with creature cards, make Old Stickfingers bigger each time you cast it, reanimate threats.

## Mana Rocks

- Golgari Locket
- Darksteel Ingot
- Worn Powerstone
- Hedron Archive

## Ramp

- Llanowar Elves
- Elvish Mystic
- Fyndhorn Elves
- Wood Elves + — fetch a Forest onto the battlefield
- Sakura-Tribe Elder + — sacrifice to fetch a basic land
- Solemn Simulacrum — ramp + draw

## Lands

- Overgrown Tomb
- Darkbore Pathway

## Self-Mill & Graveyard Fillers

- Altar of Dementia — sacrifice a creature to mill any player
- Spider Spawning — make Spider tokens equal to creatures in your graveyard
- Buried Alive — put three creatures directly into your graveyard
- Lotleth Troll + — discard creature cards to grow it
- Boneyard Wurm — power/toughness equals creatures in your graveyard
- Ghoultree + — often costs just 1 mana with a full graveyard
- Jarad, Golgari Lich Lord + — sacrifice a creature to deal damage, grows with graveyard count

## Reanimation

- Animate Dead + — return a creature from any graveyard
- Reanimate — return any creature from any graveyard to your battlefield
- Ever After + — return two creatures from your graveyard
- Bond of Revival + — return a creature with haste

## Removal

- Putrefy + — destroy any artifact or creature
- Assassin's Trophy — destroy any permanent, opponent gets a basic land
- Abrupt Decay — destroy any nonland permanent with mana value 3 or less, can't be countered
